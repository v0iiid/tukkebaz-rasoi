Our Story

Where the Mountains
Taste Like Home

Tukkebaz Rasoi was born from a simple belief — that the best food comes from honest hands, fresh soil, and a warm hearth. Perched in the serene village of Supi, Uttarakhand, our café is a sanctuary for those seeking real flavors amidst the Himalayan air.

We draw from generations of Pahadi culinary traditions — slow-cooked dals, hand-rolled rotis, seasonal saag, and chai brewed with mountain herbs. Every dish is an act of love, prepared with locally sourced ingredients and zero shortcuts.

Whether you're a traveler seeking warmth or a local craving comfort, Tukkebaz Rasoi welcomes you to our table.

— The Rasoi Family

Terms of Service — Tukkebaz Rasoi

Last updated: January 2025

General

By visiting Tukkebaz Rasoi or placing an order through any of our channels (in-person, WhatsApp, Instagram), you agree to abide by these terms. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone for reasons we deem appropriate.

Food & Allergens

While we take all reasonable care, our kitchen handles a range of ingredients including nuts, dairy, gluten, and spices. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions or allergies before ordering. We cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.

Reservations

Reservations can be made via WhatsApp or Instagram DM. A reservation is confirmed only upon our explicit acknowledgment. We recommend booking in advance for weekends and holidays.

Pricing

All prices are in Indian Rupees (INR) and are inclusive of applicable taxes unless stated otherwise. Prices may change without prior notice due to seasonal availability of ingredients.

Conduct

We maintain a warm, respectful environment for all guests. Any form of disrespectful or abusive behaviour toward our staff or other guests may result in being asked to leave the premises.

Refund Policy — Tukkebaz Rasoi

Last updated: January 2025

Dine-In Orders

If you are unsatisfied with your food, please bring it to our attention immediately. We will do our best to resolve the issue by replacing the dish or offering a suitable alternative. Refunds for dine-in orders are at the discretion of the café management.

Takeaway & Pre-Orders

Once a takeaway order has been prepared, we are unable to offer refunds unless the food is found to be unfit for consumption. In such cases, please contact us within 30 minutes of receiving your order.

Cancellation Policy

Reservations cancelled more than 2 hours before the scheduled time will not incur any charges. Cancellations made within 2 hours of the reservation may not be refundable if special preparation has been undertaken.

How to Request a Refund

Contact us directly via WhatsApp at +91 93703 44539 or visit us in person. We aim to resolve all concerns fairly and promptly — your satisfaction matters to us.

No Refund Circumstances

Refunds will not be issued for change of mind after food has been served, or for orders that have been fully consumed.
